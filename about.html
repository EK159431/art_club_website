About the Different Art Clubs


Art Club
Monday // 3:00pm to 4:30pm // Room 121
Students create projects of their own choice, ranging from any medium of painting to drawing. Students are encouraged to find mediums that they like. Students have an opportunity to collaborate and create. Student's that are artists and people new to the topic are encouraged to create and learn.




Clay Club
Thursday // 3:00pm to 4:30pm // Room 124
Students who are interested in or want to work with clay. If students are taking a current clay class, they can come in and use the time to work on projects outside of class. Students will learn how to properly use and mold clay into different shapes and creations.




Graphic Arts Club
Tuesday // 3:00pm to 4:30pm // Room 102 or 163
Students with any level of knowledge are able to come and work with photoshop. Students from other classes can also come in and work on their projects. Students who don't know how to photoshop can learn how, and make different graphic art pieces.
