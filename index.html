Art Club

Art Club is a place to gather students and inspiring artists to have fun and create art. Ranging from learning skills, working with different mediums, to participating in regular and seasonal events, all these things and more are available to any students who want to join. No matter if you are experienced, or know very little about art, there is a place for you.

The Art Clubs will come together for events such as fundraisers and big projects. The clubs want to encourage all kids from each art type to work aside one another.
